The Lamp Show / 99¢ Plus Gallery Brooklyn, NY / 2016

Curated by Zoe Alexander Fisher

99¢ Plus is pleased to present The Lamp Show, an exhibition of objects made functional by their luminescence by over 30 contemporary artists and designers.

The Lamp Show is the first in a series of exhibitions at 99¢ Plus Gallery this upcoming year which focus on the home and the objects in them - the sculptural objects to which we ascribe a function - put a light/ a flower/ some food / or rest our bums in.

From clearly “designed” and functional works, to more conceptual explorations of the function of light, The Lamp Show includes the work of emerging to established artists and designers.
