TOOL TIME is a performance and object series by Aria McManus and Raine Trainor, at Motel Gallery, Jan 25th, 2016. Featured products include GiggleDrill™ with ApplyYourself Applicator™, Glitter Grind™, Sleek Support™, StapFrag™, ScrewDriver/Screw Driver™, Bubba Inch™, I Saw Myself™, Head Bang™. Filming by Avery McCarthy, photography by KangHee Kim.
